TweetCheck

On X, scroll your timeline and click TweetCheck under a post. The side panel opens with analysis (still demo / mock data until your backend is connected).

  • Corroboration — do allow-listed trusted outlets agree, stay quiet, or disagree? (Not the same as “true/false.”)
  • AI-generation risk — plain-language heads-up about wording; not proof of who wrote it.
  • Source traceability — how easy it would be to check claims against real sources.
  • Credible alternatives — other trusted outlets on the same topic so you can compare coverage.

Mock data only: posts that mention breaking or hoax switch demo scenarios.
